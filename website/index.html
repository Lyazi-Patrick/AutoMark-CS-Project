About the App

AutoGrader is an AI-powered mobile and web application that helps teachers automatically grade students' handwritten answer scripts using OCR technology. The app matches scanned text against marking guides and instantly generates scores.

How to Use

  • Download the AutoGrader mobile app or visit our web app.
  • Sign up or log in with your email.
  • Use your phone camera to scan students' scripts.
  • Check the results and export reports as PDF.
  • Teachers can edit answer keys and marking guides in the app.
